1. Data Upload

Users are permitted to upload files of the following types: documents, images, videos, and audio files.

The maximum file size for uploads is 5 GB per file. Contact the administrator for exceptions.

2. Data Handling

Data should not be shared with external parties without explicit permission from the data owner.

Sensitive data must be encrypted before uploading and labeled appropriately.

3. Data Retention

Data will be retained for a period of 2 years unless specified otherwise by the data owner.

Users must review and purge unnecessary files on a monthy base to conserve storage space and maintain organization.
